## Lessons Learned

### X / shipordie.club

**Two-beat structure for tool launches:**

- **Line 1 (hook):** the real moment or outcome — the interesting thing that makes a stranger pause. One sentence, no filler.
- **Line 2 (context + CTA):** name the project, say what it is plainly, one concrete detail (number of examples, what it does). End with bare URL to the tool page.
- **For tool launches: link to the tool page**, not the log post
- **Single CTA only** — don't say "read the log post AND check out the tool page". Pick one destination.
- **Same post works for X and shipordie.club** — don't over-differentiate
- No hashtags. No emojis unless they add meaning.
- **Capitalized sentences.** Earlier posts were lowercase throughout. As of March 2026, sentences are capitalized normally. The register is still direct and minimal — capitalization is the only change.

### Reddit

**Structure for tool launches (Showcase flair):**

```
[2-3 sentences of personal context — who you made it for, what you observed. First person, conversational. No jargon.]

[The moment or proof point — what happened, what people said or did. Specific details over vague claims.]

[Why you're building it — the natural transition from "I kept doing this as a favor" to "so I built a tool." One sentence.]

[One-line CTA + bare URL to the tool page.]
```

Rules:

- No em dashes. Reddit spam filters flag them. Use a comma, a period, or a new sentence.
- No closing question. It reads as phony. Let the post stand on its own.
- r/relationship_advice rarely fits a tool launch unless there's a direct relationship/gift angle with a real outcome to show
- Title: plain and direct. State what you built. No clickbait, no question marks.
